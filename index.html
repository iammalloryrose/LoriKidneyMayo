Frequently Asked: Is Living Donation Safe? Living kidney donation is a well-established and safe procedure. Donors undergo thorough medical and psychological evaluations to ensure they are healthy and fully informed. Most donors are able to return to their normal lives within weeks, and studies show that living with one kidney does not decrease life expectancy or quality of life. All medical costs related to the donation are typically covered by the recipient’s insurance, and support is available throughout the entire process. If you're curious or even just considering the possibility, exploring the information is the first step — no obligation, just understanding.
